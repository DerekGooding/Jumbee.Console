License notice for Natural Earth map data
-----------------------------------------
https://www.naturalearthdata.com/
(map data embedded at src/Jumbee.Console/Geo/)

Geo/land_110m.txt — land polygons, used by the Globe control to bake its
  land/ocean mask. Converted from ne_110m_land.geojson, obtained from
  https://github.com/nvkelso/natural-earth-vector (geojson/ne_110m_land.geojson),
  by flattening every Polygon/MultiPolygon ring to one "lon,lat" per line with a
  blank line between closed rings, and rounding coordinates to 2 decimal places.

Geo/world_high.txt, Geo/world_low.txt — coastline sample points, used by the
  Canvas WorldMap shape. Natural Earth coastline data, obtained via the map data
